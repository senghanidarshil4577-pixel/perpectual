ABOUT PARTY CRUISERS LIMITED

Three Decades of Defining the Extraordinary

Founded in 1994 by Zuzer and Rachana Lucknowala, Party Cruisers Limited was built on a singular, unwavering conviction — that every guest should feel like the most important person in the room.

Thirty years later, this philosophy is powered by three specialist divisions — House of Vivaah, Vows Vachan, and Event Factory — an integrated ecosystem where distinct crafts converge to master every dimension of luxury event management.

Now an NSE-listed powerhouse headquartered in Mumbai, our footprint spans India and the Middle East — with offices across Pune, Nashik, Goa, Hyderabad, Lucknow, and Delhi, and a Dubai hub serving Doha, Bahrain, and Oman.

House of Vivaah — Design · Décor · Production

The décor is the first impression your guests receive and the final memory they carry home. As PCL's flagship design house, House of Vivaah functions as a couture studio for the wedding world. Our team of floral artisans and production specialists treat every brief as an original work of art.

Whether it is a mandap framed by floating doves or a desert odyssey featured in Vogue, we operate at a scale where imagination meets flawless execution. With an exclusive in-house inventory, we ensure that every celebration remains an unrepeatable original.

Accolades: Multiple Platinum & Gold honours for Best Décor, Sangeet, and Mehendi (2019–2023).

Vows Vachan — The Wedding Curators

Planning a wedding is deeply emotional — and you shouldn't have to navigate it alone. Vows Vachan, PCL's dedicated wedding planning and curation division, partners with you from the first conversation to the final farewell, managing every detail so you can truly be present for your celebration.

From intimate ceremonies in Goa to grand destination weddings in Rajasthan, our experienced curators bring clarity, creativity, and calm to every moment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Party Cruisers Limited — from planning luxury destination weddings to executing large-scale events across India and the Middle East.

What does Party Cruisers Limited do?
Party Cruisers Limited (PCL) is India's premier luxury event management company, operating since 1994. We specialise in bespoke weddings, luxury destination weddings, and corporate events across India and the Middle East — through three dedicated specialist brand divisions: House of Vivaah, Vows Vachan, and Event Factory.
Is Party Cruisers Limited a listed company?
Yes. Party Cruisers Limited has been listed on the National Stock Exchange (NSE) of India since 2021, making it India's first and only publicly listed event management company. This brings a level of transparency, governance, and accountability that is unique in the industry.
What are PCL's three brand divisions?
PCL operates through three specialist brands: House of Vivaah (wedding design, décor & production), Vows Vachan (end-to-end wedding planning & curation), and Event Factory (corporate event management & impression management).
How much does a luxury wedding planner in Mumbai cost?
Luxury wedding planning in Mumbai varies based on scale, services, and the number of events involved. A full-service luxury package at Party Cruisers Limited is tailored to each client's vision and guest count. We recommend reaching out for a personalised consultation and quote.
Does Party Cruisers Limited plan destination weddings?
Absolutely. Destination weddings are one of our specialties, both domestically — in Goa, Rajasthan, Hyderabad, and beyond — and internationally across the UAE, Qatar, Bahrain, and Oman. Our Vows Vachan division manages destination weddings from logistics to last-minute details.
How do I get started with Party Cruisers Limited?
Simply fill in the enquiry form above, WhatsApp us at +91 84336 59225, or reach out through any of our social channels. Our team will respond within 24 hours to schedule a consultation and guide you to the right brand division for your event.
