Who We Are

Party Cruisers Limited

Events · Weddings · Experiences

As Party Cruisers Limited, listed on the NSE since 2021, we are a collective that brings you the most luxe events you've experienced — without having to worry if your guests are having an engaging and personalized event.

Since 1994, we've expanded to a range of event services apart from our expertise in wedding curation and execution, focused on end-to-end management by working with eminent service providers across India and the Middle East.

Our Vision

Become the go-to global destination for all things wedding and events.

Our Mission

Structuring and facilitating the global events/wedding industry with a focus on transparency and collaboration.
